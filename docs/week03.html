    • If the person is at least 4 years old but less than 8, print a message that the person is an infant.
    • If the person is at least 8 years old but less than 13, print a message that the person is a child.
    • If the person is at least 13 years old but less than 20, print a message that the person is a teenager.
    • If the person is at least 20 years old, print a message that the person is an adult.
